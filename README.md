This repository contains coursework, laboratory experiments, circuit simulations, and design projects completed for ELE404 at Toronto Metropolitan University. The course focused on the analysis and design of analog electronic circuits, including diodes, BJTs, MOSFETs, amplifiers, and transistor biasing techniques.

Topics covered include:

Semiconductor device fundamentals
BJT and MOSFET amplifier design
Small-signal analysis
Frequency response of amplifiers
Operational amplifier basics
Analog circuit simulation and testing

Projects and labs involved practical circuit implementation, debugging, simulation using SPICE tools, and performance analysis of analog systems.
